SYSTEM 01 — PROSPECT TARGETING
Find the right people first.
Sending 100 DMs to the wrong people gets zero results. Sending 20 DMs to the right people gets 3–4 replies. Research before you reach out.
GREEN LIGHT — CONTACT IMMEDIATELY
5,000 – 500,000 followers
Big enough to afford you. Small enough to need you. Under 5K they don't have budget yet. Over 500K they already have a team.
Posting 3–5x per week consistently
Consistency = intent. They're serious about content. If their last post was 2 weeks ago — skip them.
Business, Finance, or Lifestyle niche
Your three niches only. You know these audiences, their hooks, their content structure. Stay in your lane.
Visibly weak editing
Shaky cuts, no captions, weak hooks, long dead air, no text overlays. This is your opening. You can see exactly what to fix before you even send the message.
Selling something
Course, coaching, product, service. They have revenue coming in — which means they have budget to reinvest. Always check the bio and link.
RED FLAG — SKIP THESE
Under 1,000 followers
Not ready. No budget, no urgency, no investment in growth yet.
Last post was 3+ weeks ago
Not serious. They'll say yes and then never send footage.
Already has polished professional editing
Hard sell. They already solved the problem you're offering to solve.
Only personal content, no monetisation angle
No product or service = no budget to invest in editing.
WHERE TO FIND THEM
TIKTOK
Search your niche → sort by Most Recent. Find creators posting raw unedited content. Check posting frequency on profile.
INSTAGRAM
Check the Suggested tab in your niche. Follow 10 creators in your niche → Instagram shows you more like them.
YOUTUBE SHORTS
Search "[niche] tips". Find channels with good content ideas but weak production. Sort by Upload Date for recent activity.
30-SECOND PRE-DM RESEARCH CHECKLIST
◈Watch 3 recent videos — note ONE specific thing you'd fix first
◈Check engagement rate — are people watching, commenting, saving?
◈Read their bio and link — what are they selling?
◈Note their biggest visible content problem — hook, pacing, captions, audio?
◈Check posting frequency — are they consistent?
The one rule: Every DM must reference something specific from their actual content. Generic DMs get ignored. A message that says "I noticed your hook in your Tuesday video loses people at second 3" gets opened every time.
SYSTEM 02 — DM SCRIPTS
3 types. Rotate them.
Never send the same DM type twice to the same person. Each type serves a different situation. Read the "When to use" before sending.
TYPE 01 — OBSERVATION DM HIGHEST VOLUME
Cold first touch — no video
Use when: You noticed something specific about their content. Fast to send. Works at scale. Target: 5–8 per day.
SCRIPT — COPY AND PERSONALISE
DM TYPE 1 — OBSERVATION Hey [Name] — Watched your last few videos on [topic]. The content itself is solid — [specific thing, e.g. "the hook on your investing video"] actually stopped my scroll. One thing I kept noticing — [specific problem, e.g. "the dead air between cuts is losing people before the payoff"]. I edit for [their niche] creators and that's exactly the kind of thing I fix. Worth a 10-minute call? — Rashed
RULES FOR THIS DM
◈Max 5 lines. No more. Every word earns its place.
◈ONE specific observation — not general praise like "great content"
◈ONE specific problem — not a list of everything wrong
◈ONE soft ask — a 10-minute call, not "hire me"
✕Never open with "Hi I'm a video editor looking for clients"
✕Never include pricing in the first message
✕Never say "I can help you grow" — it's vague and sounds like spam
TYPE 02 — VIDEO AUDIT DM HIGHEST CONVERTING
Cold first touch — with screen recording
Use when: You have 10 minutes to record a personalised audit. Target: 3 per week (Mon / Wed / Fri). Reply rates up to 30% vs 5% for text-only DMs.
Tool to use: ScreenPal (free, Windows + Mac) or Screen Studio (Mac only, premium quality). Do NOT use Loom — pricing changed in 2026 after Atlassian acquisition, now expensive for solo operators.
THE 90-SECOND AUDIT STRUCTURE
0–10sec
Open on their profile
"Hey [Name], I was watching your content and I want to show you something specific I noticed..." — Show their channel on screen as you say this.
10–45sec
Screen share their actual video
"See this moment at [timestamp] — this is where you're losing people. The [specific problem] means the hook never lands. Here's what I'd do instead..." — Be specific. Point at the actual frame.
45–75sec
Show a quick example from your portfolio
"This is similar to what I did for a [creator type] — you can see how [specific fix] keeps people watching through to the CTA." — Show one before/after from your own content.
75–90sec
Soft close
"I'd love to do this for your whole channel. If you want to jump on a 10-minute call I can show you exactly what I'd change first." — One ask. Stop recording immediately after.
DM THAT GOES WITH IT
DM TYPE 2 — VIDEO AUDIT Hey [Name] — I recorded a 90-second breakdown of your [most recent video] — showing one specific thing I'd fix that would immediately improve your retention. [ScreenPal link] No pitch. Just the observation. Let me know what you think. — Rashed
TYPE 03 — WARM DM EASIEST CLOSE
They've already engaged with you
Use when: They liked your post, commented, followed your account, or replied to your story. They already know you exist — move fast, this window closes quickly.
SCRIPT
DM TYPE 3 — WARM Hey [Name] — noticed you [liked / followed / commented on] my [post / reel]. Checked out your channel — you're doing [genuine specific observation]. I work with [their niche] creators on exactly this. Would a quick call make sense? — Rashed
Why this converts highest: They already trust you enough to engage. Your profile already did the selling. This message just opens the door they were already standing at.
THE CORE RULE: Every message must feel like it was written for one person and one person only. If you can copy-paste it to 50 people without changing a word — rewrite it. Specificity is the difference between a reply and silence.
SYSTEM 03 — VIDEO AUDIT
Your highest-converting outreach tool.
A personalised 90-second screen recording gets up to 30% reply rates. Text DMs average 5%. The effort of 10 minutes per audit is worth 6x more replies.
DO NOT USE LOOM. After the Atlassian acquisition in 2026, Loom pricing increased significantly and reliability dropped. Use ScreenPal (free, all platforms) or Screen Studio (Mac, premium quality) instead.
SETUP — DO THIS ONCE
SCREENPAL (FREE — WINDOWS + MAC)
Go to screenpal.com → Download free → Record screen + webcam → Get shareable link instantly. No video limit on free tier. This is what you use for every audit.
SCREEN STUDIO (MAC ONLY — PAID)
screenstudio.link → $89 one-time → Cinematic zoom effects, auto-highlights clicks. Makes your screen recordings look premium. Worth it if you're on Mac and doing 10+ audits/week.
BEFORE YOU RECORD — 5-MINUTE PREP
◈Open their profile and their most recent video — have it ready to share screen
◈Identify the ONE specific problem you'll show — hook timing, dead air, no captions, weak CTA
◈Have one of your own before/after examples ready to show at the 45-second mark
◈Write your opening line word for word — the first 10 seconds must be tight
✕Don't record more than 90 seconds — longer = lower completion rate = fewer replies
✕Don't show more than 1 problem — too many issues feels overwhelming not helpful
THE 90-SECOND SCRIPT TEMPLATE
AUDIO AUDIT SCRIPT — 90 SECONDS [0–10 sec] "Hey [Name] — I was going through your TikTok and I want to show you something specific I noticed in your last video..." [Show their profile on screen] [10–40 sec] "So if we look at this video here — watch what happens at second [X]..." [Screen share their video, pause at the problem moment] "You can see [specific problem]. This is where most viewers are dropping off. The fix is actually simple..." [Show or describe the fix clearly] [40–70 sec] "Here's an example — this is work I did for a similar creator in [their niche]..." [Show your before/after portfolio piece] "Same structure, different edit — watch time went from [X] to [Y]." [70–90 sec] "I'd love to do a proper audit of your whole channel. If you want to jump on a 10-minute call this week I can show you exactly what I'd change first. Link in my bio." [Stop recording immediately]
After recording: Watch it back once. If you said "um" more than 3 times in the first 10 seconds — re-record. First impressions decide whether they watch the rest. After that, send it. Don't overthink it.
SYSTEM 04 — FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE
Most closes happen on follow-up.
The first DM plants a seed. The follow-up sequence is where conversions actually happen. 4 touches maximum. After 4 with no reply — move on.
The rule: Maximum 4 touches. Never contact the same person twice in the same week. Space each follow-up exactly as shown below. After touch 4 with no reply — mark as Dead and move on. Chasing beyond 4 damages your reputation.

OBJECTION RESPONSES
"IT'S TOO EXPENSIVE"
"I get that. Can I ask — what are you currently spending on editing, and what results are you getting from it?"
Reframe price as ROI, not cost. One client conversion pays for months of editing.
"I NEED TO THINK ABOUT IT"
"Of course. What specifically is making you pause — is it the investment, the scope, or the timing?"
Never accept a vague no. Find the real objection — then address that one specifically.
"I ALREADY HAVE AN EDITOR"
"That's great. What made you get on this call today?"
They reached out for a reason. Find it. An existing editor doesn't mean a satisfied one.
"CAN YOU DO A FREE TRIAL EDIT?"
"I don't do trial edits — but I'm happy to walk you through my recent work for [their niche] and talk through exactly what I'd do with your content."
Never work for free. Show proof instead. Your portfolio from the first 30 days is exactly for this moment.

SYSTEM 06 — PROSPECT TRACKER
Track every single contact.
If it's not logged, it didn't happen. The tracker tells you who to follow up with, when, and what to say. Build this in Notion or a spreadsheet — use this as your template.
STATUS DEFINITIONS
CONTACTED
DM sent. Waiting for reply. Check daily.
REPLIED
They responded. Move fast — book a call within 24 hours.
CALL BOOKED
Call scheduled. Prepare with the sales system. Research their content again the day before.
DEAD
4 touches, no reply. Move on. Don't contact again for at least 90 days.
CLOSED
Client signed. Move to onboarding. Remove from outreach tracker.
NO SHOW
Booked a call and didn't show. Send one follow-up message only. Then Dead.
